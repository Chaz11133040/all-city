Brisbanes Legal Art Wall

Get your paint on, pig approved

Looking for somewhere to paint? Why not try Brisbane's legal street art wall down in Ekibin Park, Greenslopes way. It's a fun place; hang out under the overpass or trek down into the stormwater tunnels. A few things to keep in mind, though. First, if the cops don't like your style, they'll come rub it off. Also, don't go painting over anyone's piece unless you're sure you can do a better job. Lastly, it might be wise not to put up your usual tag on your work here. Rumor has it the cops like to keep an eye out around here to see who's doing what. If they see you throwing up something pretty and they find that name on some housewife's fence, they know who to look for. Here's some of our favorite art that's down there now.
